SUSTAINABILITY

Discover EABL’S planS to help create A sustainable East Africa

We contribute to the UN Sustainable Development Goals and broader global sustainable development agenda by building resilient supply chains, productive communities, and a healthier environment.

We have focused on contributing progress toward the goals by using our reach and partnerships to drive meaningful positive change.

  • Agribusiness

    Our crop financing and risk mitigation strategy protects our farmers from losses from unforeseen weather hazards.

  • Governance & Ethics

    EABL is an active participant in the Global Compact Network Kenya.

  • Responsible Drinking

    We acknowledge that, like many other products, when misused, alcohol can lead to individual and social harm.

  • Safety First

    Our core values: ‘Valuing each other’, ‘Pride in what we do’ and ‘Being the best.

  • Water & Energy Stewardship

    The EABL Kenya site continues to be the most efficient, holding the first position with regards to delivering the lowest cost conversion cost per litre of beer produced.

  • Environmental Conservation

    At EABL, we recognize that environmental issues are critical to the sustenance of our business.

We have longstanding and fruitful partnerships with farmers on whose raw materials we rely on. This is because we are committed to growing value in the communities where we operate.

As such, we work with local agricultural communities to support them grow our raw materials, which ensures a steady and long-term supply of our ingredients. In turn, the farmers are assured of high yields, a ready market for their produce, and timely payments. Where appropriate, we help equip them with the knowledge, tools, and specialist support to develop sustainable agriculture.

Our crop financing and risk mitigation strategy protects our farmers from losses from unforeseen weather hazards. To date, we have contracted more than 47,000 sorghum and barley farmers. These farmers receive over KShs. 2 billion annually and help us attain our goal of sourcing 85% of our raw materials locally.

We partner with both small and large-scale farmers, women and youth farmers, and entrepreneurs. This approach is not only changing the lives of individual farmers but also transforming entire communities.
